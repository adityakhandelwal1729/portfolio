Event Coordinator — ECE Graduate Student Organization

As an active member of the ECE Graduate Student Organization, I helped organize academic and social initiatives designed to foster community and collaboration among graduate students in Electrical and Computer Engineering. I coordinated Ph.D. practice qualifying exams to support academic preparedness and professional growth within the department.

Beyond academics, I contributed to planning and hosting social activities—including happy hours, amusement park outings, and cultural celebrations—that strengthened connections across cohorts and promoted a vibrant, inclusive graduate student community.
