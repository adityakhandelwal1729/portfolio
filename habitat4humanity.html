Volunteer Builder — Habitat for Humanity, Denver, Colorado

As a volunteer with Habitat for Humanity in Denver, I contributed to the construction of affordable homes for families in need, working alongside homeowners and community members to promote long-term housing stability. My role involved hands-on participation in framing, insulation, and finishing work while collaborating with diverse volunteer teams committed to the mission of building strength, stability, and self-reliance through shelter.

Through this experience, I gained a greater appreciation for sustainable community development and the collective impact of volunteer-driven efforts in addressing housing insecurity and empowering families to build better futures.
