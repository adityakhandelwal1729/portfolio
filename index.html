About Me

My name is Aditya (pronounced ah-dit-yuh — though I welcome any creative variations; collecting them has become an interesting quest). I am originally from Akola, Maharashtra, and I am currently based in Pittsburgh, Pennsylvania, where I am completing my Master of Science in Electrical and Computer Engineering at Carnegie Mellon University. My idea of fun includes hiking, fitness activities, and roller skating whenever I get the chance. I also enjoy exploring new ideas across both human sciences—such as psychology, medicine, and neuroscience—and technology, particularly the hardware stack spanning fabrication, devices, circuits, and VLSI, with an emphasis on translational applications.
